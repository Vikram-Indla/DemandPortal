-----------------------------------
1. In Replit, navigate to the "github-upload" folder (you're here now!)

   - README.md
   - SETUP_GUIDE.md

STEP 2: Download Files to Your Computer
----------------------------------------
Option A: Download individual files
   - Right-click each file/folder → "Download"
   
Option B: Download the archive (recommended)
   - In the root folder, find: roadmap-dashboard-upload.tar.gz
   - Right-click → Download
   - Extract it on your computer

STEP 3: Upload to GitHub
-------------------------
1. Go to: https://github.com/demandportal/roadmap-dashboard
2. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
3. Drag ALL files and folders into the upload area
4. Commit message: "Initial dashboard import - 6 specialized views"
5. Click "Commit changes"
